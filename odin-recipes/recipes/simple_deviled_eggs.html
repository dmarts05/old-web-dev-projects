Simple Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are ingenious. They take hard-boiled eggs — already one of the world's most perfect foods — and elevate them into an elegant bite that's worthy of any appetizer plate. From summer picnics and Christmas or Easter brunch, to afternoon snack time and Sunday supper, deviled eggs are more than up for the occasion.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks
  2. set whites aside
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl
  4. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, onion, and celery; mix well
  5. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites
  6. Sprinkle with paprika
  7. Refrigerate until serving
